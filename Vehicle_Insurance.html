FAQ

Aapke Sawal — Hamare Jawab

NCB (No Claim Bonus) kya hota hai?
NCB ek discount hai jo aapko milti hai agar aapne pichle saal koi claim nahi kiya. Yeh 20% se shuru hokar 50% tak ho sakti hai. Renewal pe automatically apply hoti hai — apna NCB zaaya mat karein choti claims ke liye.
IDV kya hota hai?
IDV yaani Insured Declared Value — aapki gaadi ki current market value. Total loss ya chhori hone par yahi amount milti hai. IDV sahi set karwayein — na zyada, na kam.
Zero Depreciation sab logon ko lena chahiye?
Zero Dep especially new gaadi ke liye best hai (0-5 saal). Purani gaadi ke liye regular comprehensive bhi kaam karta hai. Premium thoda zyada hota hai par claim mein full value milti hai — long term mein faaydemand hai.
Renewal miss ho gayi — kya karna chahiye?
Turant Jai Prakash ji ko call karein! Grace period khatam hone ke baad gaadi inspect karni padti hai. Jitni jaldi renew karein utna better — bina insurance ke gaadi chalana illegal hai aur fine bhi lag sakta hai.
Flood mein gaadi doob gayi — cover hoga?
Haan! Comprehensive aur Zero Dep plans mein natural calamity — flood, cyclone, earthquake — sab cover hota hai. Engine protection add-on lena na bhoolein — engine ko flood damage mein alag cover milti hai.
