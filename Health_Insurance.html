FAQ

Aapke Sawal — Hamare Jawab

Health insurance lene ki sahi umar kya hai?
Jitni jaldi lein utna behtar! 25-35 saal ki umar mein premium sabse kam hota hai aur waiting period bhi pehle puri ho jaati hai. Umar badhne ke saath premium badh jaata hai aur kuch conditions cover nahi hoti.
Pre-existing disease cover hoti hai?
Haan, lekin ek waiting period ke baad — generally 2-4 saal. Senior Citizen plans mein yeh 1-2 saal bhi ho sakta hai. Isliye jaldi lena faaydemand hai.
Cashless aur reimbursement mein kya farq hai?
Cashless mein network hospital mein aapko kuch nahi bharna — insurance company seedha hospital ko deti hai. Reimbursement mein aap pehle pay karte ho phir company wapas karta hai. Hum cashless hospitals dhundhne mein help karte hain.
Kitni sum insured leni chahiye?
Delhi mein minimum ₹5 Lakh family ke liye recommended hai. Agar budget ho toh ₹10-15 Lakh behtar hai. Major surgery aur critical illness mein yahi kaam aata hai. Super top-up se aap zyada cover kam premium mein le sakte hain.
Company ka health insurance hai toh alag lena chahiye?
Bilkul lena chahiye! Company insurance naukri chhootne pe band ho jaata hai. Personal plan hamesha active rehta hai. Company cover usually ₹3-5L hota hai jo kaafi nahi hota. Super top-up se extra cover bahut kam mein milta hai.
